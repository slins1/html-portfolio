My top 3 favorite movies

(at least right now, this changes frequently)

Spirited Away

Hardly an original pick, but goodness gracious are those visuals gorgeous. I always choke up watching this movie, and it's a toss-up whether the animation or the score is the thing that pushes me over the edge during that particular viewing.

The Princess Bride

A big part of this is the nostalgia I carry for watching it with my family growing up, but honestly? This one has everything. Swashbuckling, comedy, vengeance, twue wove...if you can't find something to like in The Princess Bride, there's no pleasing you.

Speed Racer

So so SO much better than I think it gets credit for. In an age where studios are rushing to do live-action adaptations of animated movies and shows, this is one of the only ones I can think of that actually does a good job of embracing its cartoony origins in its visual effects. Plus, it's just fun. I value that way more in my movies than any "artsiness".
